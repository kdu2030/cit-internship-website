What our students are capable of

Programming

CIT students have extensive experience in both Java and Python. In addition, CIT students have also been exposed to Structured Query Languages.

Web Design and Development

CIT students are also proficient at web design. They have mastered HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python to suit any organization's web development needs.

Project Management

Each CIT student is knowledgeable about both traditional project managment and agile project management. They have used various project management techniques, notably SCRUM, to complete team projects for non-profit organizations.





Mission Statement

The mission of the CIT Internship course is to provide students with an opportunity to gain valuable experience and perspective in the workplace for a future IT career or profession. CIT students will be expected to apply knowledge gained through observation and participation in a professional setting, and are encouraged to seek activities that expand their personal talent and interest.
