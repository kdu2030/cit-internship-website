About the Center of Information Technology

The Center of Information Technology is the Henrico County Specialty Center at Deep Run High School. At the CIT, students have the opportunity to receive a technology based education that will prepare them for a computer science or IT career in the future. Each CIT student has experience in Programming, Web Design/Development, and Database Management. In addition, each CIT student has experience with project management techniques from Traditional Project Management to User Centered Design to SCRUM and Agile. Every CIT student has also applied his or her knowledge in CIT projects, where CIT students create software/hardware for local nonprofits.

About the CIT Internship Program

The CIT Internship Program is a course that allows Deep Run CIT students to apply the skills they've learned in a professional environment to get a unique perspective for a future IT career. In addition, CIT students will be able to expand and explore their own personal talents and interests relating to the IT field. The course runs from June 24 to August 16, but students can start at their internship as early as June 1.
